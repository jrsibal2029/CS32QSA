HOTELS & ACCOMMODATIONS

CORON WESTOWN RESORT

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of Coron, Palawan, Coron Westown Resort serves as a tranquil sanctuary for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure. Just a short distance from the vibrant town center, this resort uniquely combines comfort with accessibility, making it an excellent base for exploring the breathtaking attractions that Coron has to offer. With lush gardens, a sparkling swimming pool, and spacious accommodations, the resort provides an inviting atmosphere that feels like a home away from home. Whether you're soaking up the sun by the pool, savoring delicious regional cuisine at the on-site restaurant, or planning excursions to iconic destinations like Kayangan Lake and Twin Lagoon, Coron Westown Resort ensures that your stay is both rejuvenating and memorable. Experience the warm hospitality and picturesque surroundings that make this resort a favorite among visitors to Coron.

The resort features a variety of well-appointed rooms designed for comfort and relaxation, each equipped with modern amenities such as plush beds, air conditioning, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Guests can unwind in the inviting ambiance, whether lounging by the expansive swimming pool or enjoying a leisurely meal at the on-site restaurant, which offers a delightful selection of local and international dishes. With its friendly staff dedicated to providing exceptional service, Coron Westown Resort ensures that every guest feels welcomed and at home.

PEARL VISTA DE CORON RESORT HOTEL

Pearl Vista de Coron Resort is a tranquil haven set on a hillside overlooking the breathtaking beauty of Coron, Palawan. This resort offers guests a picturesque getaway, seamlessly combining stunning natural landscapes with modern comfort. Conveniently located just a short distance from the town center, Pearl Vista provides easy access to various attractions, including enchanting lagoons, vibrant coral reefs, and the fascinating historical sites of Coron.

The resort features a diverse range of accommodations, from cozy standard rooms to spacious suites, all thoughtfully designed for comfort and relaxation. Each room is equipped with essential amenities, such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private balconies that showcase unparalleled views of the surrounding mountains and the beautiful sea. Guests can indulge in delightful dining options at the on-site restaurant, which offers a menu of local and international dishes crafted from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. After a day of exploration, guests can unwind by the inviting swimming pool or stroll through the lush gardens.
