WHY WE CHOSE CORON

We chose Coron for its unparalleled natural beauty, diverse marine life, and rich cultural heritage, making it an ideal destination for adventure and relaxation. Known for its stunning landscapes, pristine lagoons, and notable World War II shipwrecks, Coron offers a unique blend of history and breathtaking scenery. Furthermore, the warmth and hospitality of its local communities enhance the overall experience, inviting travelers to immerse themselves in the enchanting charm of this Palawan gem.
